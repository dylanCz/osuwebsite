Game Settings

  • Mode: Stock
  • Stocks: 4 stocks
  • Time Limit: 8:00 minutes
  • Handicap: OFF
  • Team Attack / Friendly Fire: On
  • Damage Ratio: 100%
  • Items: OFF and/or NONE
  • Pause: OFF
  • All Characters Available
  • Singles: All sets are best-of-3 until top 32, where sets are best-of-5
  • Doubles: All sets are best-of-3 until top 8, where sets are best-of-5

Set Procedure

  • Players select characters or Double Blind Character Select
  • Use 1-2-1 Stage Striking to determine the first stage
  • Players play the first game of the set
  • Winning player of the preceding match bans a stage/stages
  • Losing player of the preceding match counterpicks a stage
  • Winning player of the preceding match may change characters
  • Losing player of the preceding match may change characters
  • Players play the next game of the set
  • Repeat Steps 4 through 8 for all subsequent games
