About the Team

Hailing from all over Scotland; LockOut brings together players and tournament organisers alike to usher in a new era of Scottish Smash. The group and its members have collectively ran 20+ Smash tournaments: including various OSU, Rushdown Edinburgh and Hypespotting events. With this new collaboration, our goal is to increase the quality of events within Scotland and to provide a new, fresh experience for those travelling to the North.
